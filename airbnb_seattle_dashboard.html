Key Findings
Pricing Insight
ZIP 98134 (SoDo/Industrial) commands the highest avg price at $206.6/night — driven by proximity to waterfront & event venues, not traditional tourism.
Seasonality Paradox
January has the highest occupancy (43.7%) despite not being peak tourist season — conference & business travel inflates demand. Summer shows highest prices but not occupancy.
Supply Structure
1-bedroom units dominate supply at 63% of listings (2,417) yet earn the lowest avg price ($95.7). Studios actually earn more per night — suggesting 1BR hosts are under-pricing.
Review Seasonality
August review volume (13,018) is 4.5× higher than February (2,643). Joining Calendar + Reviews datasets reveals true demand patterns invisible in pricing data alone.
